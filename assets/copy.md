# Aawax — copy

**Hero tagline:** Speak like you mean it.
**Hero sub:** Aawax is your pocket speaking coach — it listens, nudges, and cheers you on.

## Features

1. **Real-time feedback**
   Aawax listens while you talk and flags filler words, runaway pace, and the pauses that land — live, as you speak.

2. **Drills that adapt**
   Short practice sessions tuned to your goal, whether it's a wedding toast or a board-room pitch. They get harder as you get better.

3. **See yourself improve**
   Every session is scored and tracked, so the progress you feel is progress you can actually watch.
